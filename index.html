Siamese Cats

Siamese cats are known for their sleek bodies, striking blue almond-shaped eyes, and sociable personalities. They are very vocal and enjoy human companionship.

Maine Coon

Maine Coons are one of the largest domestic cat breeds, famous for their tufted ears, bushy tails, and friendly, playful nature.

Persian Cats

Persian cats have long, luxurious fur and a calm temperament. They require regular grooming to maintain their beautiful coats.

Ragdoll Cats

Ragdolls are large, affectionate cats known for going limp when picked up, earning their name. They have striking blue eyes and semi-long fur.

Bengal Cats

Bengal cats are energetic and playful, boasting a wild appearance with leopard-like spots and rosettes on their coats.

Sphynx Cats

The Sphynx breed is famous for its almost hairless body, large ears, and affectionate, outgoing personality.

Scottish Fold

Scottish Folds are noted for their distinctive folded ears and sweet, calm demeanor, making them excellent companions.

British Shorthair

British Shorthairs are sturdy cats with dense, plush coats and round faces. They tend to be easygoing and friendly.

Abyssinian Cats

Abyssinians are one of the oldest known breeds, recognized for their ticked coat and lively, playful personalities.

Oriental Shorthair

Oriental Shorthairs have slender bodies, large ears, and come in many coat colors and patterns. They are curious and talkative.

Norwegian Forest Cat

These cats have thick, water-resistant coats and a strong build, adapted for cold climates. They are friendly and independent.

Burmese Cats

Burmese cats are muscular and affectionate with short, shiny coats and large expressive eyes.

Turkish Van

Turkish Vans are semi-longhaired cats known for their love of water and distinctive colored markings on the head and tail.

Savannah Cats

Savannah cats are a hybrid breed with wild serval ancestry, featuring tall frames and large ears with spotted coats.

American Shorthair

American Shorthairs have strong, muscular bodies and a variety of coat colors. They are known for their gentle and easygoing nature.

Exotic Shorthair

Exotic Shorthairs have a plush, dense coat like Persians but with shorter hair, combining appearance with ease of grooming.

Russian Blue

Known for their dense, silvery-blue coat and vivid green eyes, Russian Blues are gentle, reserved, and intelligent cats.

Siberian Cats

Siberians have thick, water-resistant fur and are native to Russia. They are agile, friendly, and adapt well to families.

Chartreux Cats

Chartreux cats have woolly blue-gray coats and copper or gold eyes. They are quiet, sweet-natured, and good hunters.

Manx Cats

Manx cats are tailless or have very short tails. They are playful, intelligent, and have a strong hunting instinct.

Devon Rex

Devon Rex cats have large ears, short wavy fur, and a mischievous, affectionate personality.

Cornish Rex

Cornish Rex cats have soft, curly coats and a slender build. They are active, playful, and love attention.

Singapura Cats

Singapura is one of the smallest cat breeds, known for large eyes and ears and a curious, gentle temperament.

Havana Brown

Havana Browns have sleek, rich brown coats and distinctive green eyes. They are social, playful, and intelligent cats.

Balinese Cats

Balinese cats are similar to Siamese cats but with longer silky fur. They are vocal, affectionate, and intelligent.
